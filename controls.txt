

CALIBRATION


There is a hockey puck over your right shoulder, grab it and use right stick to move seat
your shoulders should be approximately level with the blades of the mech's shoulders


ARMS


grab the control yokes and move them around. recalibrate seat if necessary
triggers activate weapons


MOVEMENT


Right stick - pitch and roll
Left stick - XY movement

Right thumb button A - up
right thumb button B - down

Left thumb button X + Right stick - yaw

Gaze over 60* triggers automatic pitch and yaw (can be disabled)

Left thumb button Y - flight mode + boost (you will go weeeee)
